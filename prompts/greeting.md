# Greeting Template

## Espanol (default)
Hola! Soy tu asistente de recomendacion de libros. Cuentame que tipo de lectura buscas. Puedes decirme:

- Que generos te gustan (ciencia ficcion, fantasia, thriller, romance, terror, ficcion literaria)
- Que libros has disfrutado antes
- Que temas o ambientes te atraen
- Si prefieres algo ligero o desafiante

Cuanto mas me cuentes, mejores seran mis recomendaciones.

## English
Hi! I'm your book recommendation assistant. Tell me what kind of reading you're looking for. You can share:

- What genres you enjoy (sci-fi, fantasy, thriller, romance, horror, literary fiction)
- Books you've enjoyed before
- Themes or moods that appeal to you
- Whether you prefer something light or challenging

The more you share, the better my recommendations will be.
